## Design Context

### Users

Primary visitors are beginners, hobby players, and students looking for badminton coaching.
They need to quickly understand training quality, class pricing, and whether the coaching environment is fun and supportive.
Primary job-to-be-done: evaluate trust, value, and fit fast, then take a clear next step (contact, register, or inquire).

### Brand Personality

Voice and tone should feel warm, encouraging, and confident.
3-word personality: funny, caring, results-driven.
Emotional goals: combine approachability and community warmth with visible professionalism and performance credibility.

### Aesthetic Direction

Visual direction: warm, community-focused, and friendly in light mode.
No direct reference sites were provided, so the design should avoid generic template aesthetics and instead emphasize clear hierarchy, real training outcomes, and a human coaching story.
SEO intent should shape structure: semantic headings, clear service descriptions, trust signals, and conversion-focused content sections.

### Design Principles

1. Lead with clarity in 5 seconds: communicate environment, quality, and price transparency above the fold.
2. Build trust before selling: show coach credibility, student progress, and concrete class details.
3. Keep a warm, human tone: community-first copy with motivating, non-intimidating language.
4. Design for action and discoverability: strong calls to action plus SEO-friendly semantic content architecture.
5. Accessibility is non-negotiable: target WCAG-compliant contrast, keyboard access, and sensible motion defaults.
